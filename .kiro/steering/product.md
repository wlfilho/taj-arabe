# Product Overview

**Card√°pio Digital** is a modern digital menu application for restaurants and food establishments. The application allows customers to browse products, add items to cart, and complete orders directly through WhatsApp integration.

## Key Features
- **Dynamic Content Management**: All content is managed through Google Sheets, eliminating the need for technical knowledge to update the menu
- **Interactive Shopping Cart**: Persistent cart with quantity adjustments and automatic total calculation
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct order completion through formatted WhatsApp messages
- **Responsive Design**: Mobile-first approach with light/dark theme support
- **Real-time Search**: Instant product search by name, description, or category
- **Lead Generation**: Coupon form that captures leads directly to Google Sheets

## Target Users
- Restaurant owners and staff who need to manage digital menus
- Customers browsing and ordering from the digital menu
- Establishments wanting WhatsApp-based order processing

## Business Model
The application serves as a complete digital menu solution that bridges the gap between traditional menu management and modern digital ordering, with Google Sheets as the content management system.
